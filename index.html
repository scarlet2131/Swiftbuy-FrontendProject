  • NFT marketplaces are where you can list your NFT for sale. They look a lot like auction houses. NFTs are auctioned off, just like real artwork, and the highest bidder wins the prize. The more people bid, the higher the price will be. This is why collections sell so easily: they create scarcity, which increases the value of the NFT and thus the price.

  • No, you are not required to be a designer to use an NFT (Non-Fungible Token) marketplace. NFT marketplaces are platforms where digital assets such as artwork, music, videos, and even virtual real estate can be purchased, sold, and traded via blockchain technology. While some users use these platforms to create and sell their own digital creations, others act as collectors or investors, buying NFTs created by artists and creators.

  • To enter the NFT marketplace using Swiftbuy, learn about NFTs and blockchain, set up a digital wallet, create or buy NFTs, list them for sale, promote via social media, and manage transactions. These steps will kickstart your journey, irrespective of your design skills.

  • Blockchain gas fees, also known simply as "gas fees," are the costs associated with making transactions or executing smart contracts on a blockchain network. Gas fees exist primarily on networks like Ethereum, which rely on a Proof of Work (PoW) or Proof of Stake (PoS) consensus mechanism.
